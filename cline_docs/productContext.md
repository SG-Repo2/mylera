# Product Context

## Purpose
MyLera is a health and fitness tracking application that integrates with platform-specific health providers (Apple HealthKit and Google Health Connect) to sync and track user health metrics.

## Problems Solved
- Seamless integration with native health platforms
- Cross-platform health data synchronization
- Unified health metrics tracking
- User-friendly health data visualization

## Core Functionality
- Health data synchronization from platform providers
- Permission management for health data access
- Metric tracking (steps, distance, calories, heart rate, etc.)
- Error handling and user feedback
- Real-time health data updates

## Target Users
- Health-conscious individuals
- Fitness enthusiasts
- Users of Apple Health and Google Fit
- People wanting to track their health metrics in one place
